🚨 Anti-Ragging Awareness

What is Ragging?

Ragging means any act that causes physical or psychological harm, creates fear, or violates the dignity of a student.

Ragging is a Criminal Offense!
  • Imprisonment up to 3 years and/or fine up to Rs. 25,000
  • Immediate suspension/expulsion from institution
  • Debarment from admission to any educational institution
📞 National Anti-Ragging Helpline: 1800-180-5522 (24x7 Toll-Free)
If You Witness Ragging:
  1. Report immediately through this portal or helpline
  2. Do not participate or be a silent spectator
  3. Support the victim and encourage them to report
  4. Remember: Silence enables the perpetrators
